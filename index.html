Thousands of decisions,
made better

Every construction project involves thousands of decisions that can directly impact business case, design, timeline, and budget. Join empowers teams to make better, more informed decisions throughout the project lifecycle, from design and preconstruction to final execution.

Cost, timeline, and design integrity, built in

Join is the only platform that organizes owners, designers and construction teams around a single source of truth – an easy-to-access repository of real-time data and decision history. Teams are able to make faster, more confident decisions on the path to cost and design certainty.

Forward progress, not never-ending email threads

Boundaries between construction phases are disappearing. Join helps to eliminate silos (and inbox bottlenecks), enabling teams to invest more in each other, creating greater transparency, uncovering opportunities, and building momentum toward a shared goal.

Visualize it. Analyze it.
Build it better.

Whether you’re swapping out light switches or an entire structural system, Join’s highly visual and team-centered platform makes it easier to incorporate critical factors like cost, timeline, design, constructability, supply chain and the owner’s business case into the decision process. It connects the right people to the right information at the time of the decision.

Better outcomes today.
Greater opportunities tomorrow.

Construction delivery methods of all kinds – from Design Build to CMAR to TVD – benefit from Join’s ability to help project teams make timely, informed decisions together. Workflows work better and projects are delivered faster and on budget.

Join becomes even more valuable over time, offering a birds-eye view of projects and portfolios that teams can use to monitor portfolio activity, estimate future jobs with precision, mitigate risk and win even more work.
